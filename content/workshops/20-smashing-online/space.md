  sub: |
     **`initial`** | **`auto`** | **`none`** | **`<grow>`**
  caption: |
    The spec is written so that

- pre: Initial
  title: |
    **Shrink**, If Necessary
  sub: Same as **`0 1 auto`**
- pre: Auto
  title: |
    **Shrink** or **Grow**
  sub: Same as **`1 1 auto`**
- pre: None
  title: |
    **Don't Flex**
  sub: Same as **`0 0 auto`**
- pre: |

  title: |
